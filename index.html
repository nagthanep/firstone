Prasad Nagthane

CEO of Google Inc.

Nagthane Prasad(born January 16, 1999), known as Prasad Nagthane, is an Indian-American business executive. He is the chief executive officer (CEO) of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC. Born in Nanded, Mharashtra, Nagthane earned his degree from the MGM'S College of Engineering in Computer Science and Engineering. Moving to the United States, he attained an M.S. from Stanford University in Computer sciences and engineering.
